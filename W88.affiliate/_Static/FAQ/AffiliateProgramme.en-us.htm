Affiliate Programme

1. What is W88 Affiliates Program?
W88 Affiliates Program is a partnership program where you earn commissions based on the wagering activity of players that you refer to any of the clients we manage. It's an easy way to generate income from traffic you already have on your website.

2. How do I sign up with W88 Affiliates Program?
The W88 Affiliate Program is free to join. All you need to do is complete the online registration form and you will be contacted within three (3) business days regarding your acceptance into the program. If your application is accepted, you receive an email containing your unique affiliate ID, giving access to banners, text links, and other content and marketing tools to add to your website.

3. What if I don't have a Website, can I still join?
To participate in the W88 Affiliates Program, you should have a Website (Live) that has online gambling as its central theme. Should you not have a website, W88 Affiliate will however review cases on an individual basis. Please email affiliate_support@w88.com for more information.

4. What if I have more than one website?
you can have multiple websites, you only need to apply once to W88 Affiliates and use the same affiliate ID for multiple sites. You can also set up separate tracking codes for each site, so you can compare how well each site performs.

5. Why might I not be accepted as an affiliate?
Every application is reviewed by W88 Affiliates prior to acceptance. W88 Affiliates will treat all applications in good faith but reserves the right to refuse any application at its absolute discretion.
