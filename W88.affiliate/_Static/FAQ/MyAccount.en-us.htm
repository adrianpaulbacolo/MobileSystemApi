My Account

1. How do I change my account information?
You can change your account information by clicking on Account and then going to Update Account details on the menu.

2. How secure is my information?
Your contact information is secure with W88 Affiliate. We do not sell or rent your contact information to anyone. Please refer to our Privacy Policy for more information.

3. I forgot my password. How do I get it?
You can request a password reminder at affiliate.w88.com. It will be sent to your email address. If you need further assistance, please email to affiliate_support@w88.com
